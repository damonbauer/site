Delete Account

In order to delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Open the GroupGifts app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Tap "Profile" in the tab bar at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Tap "Edit" in the top right corner of the screen.
  5. Tap "Delete Account"
  6. After reading the prompt, tap "OK" to delete your account.
