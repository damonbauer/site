On Air - I Launched a Product Today

Today, I "launched" my first product. On Air is a Mac menubar app that controls your "smart devices" when an event occurs (like your camera turning on).

I use it to turn a WiFi bulb on when I'm in video calls, so my family can easily tell when I can't be interrupted.
